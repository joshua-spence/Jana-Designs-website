Desk Menara

The Desk Menara was designed in response to small space living and the problems that may arise from such living conditions. Working in pairs, we were given a brief from Umbra to design in partnership with John Lewis, we were to demonstrate thinking of manufacturing processes, materials and packaging to get a well-rounded understanding of the full production process.

Efficeintly Designed

Desk Menera is a compact organiser that is designed to help keep your work surface tidy. It clamps onto any desk to offer a shelving solution for living spaces that have minimal storage or restrictions that prohibit drilling into the wall. All the wooden parts can be CNC cut out of a 780mm walnut plank with minimal wastage and the metal parts can be cut from a single 280mm steel rod. The whole process is very efficient, and all the parts fit into a compact box for the user to assemble at home.
