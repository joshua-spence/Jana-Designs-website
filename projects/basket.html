Korg

Designed for SCP’s Box Collection Korg is a multifunctional luxury basket. When used for it’s primary function the basket it can be filled with blankets or pillows for easily accessed when needed. Once flipped upside down, a cushion could be placed on top to transform it into a stool for extra seating. In addition to these two functions a lid could be added on top which would give it a table surface for books or a lamp.

hexagon tiers

Restricted to 3 cuts of beechwood I decided to make the whole basket from the 15mm x 27mm cut. Considering the complex appearance, construction for the basket was very simple. It is made from diagonal half lap joints to form the hexagons. Then once I made 5 tiers, they were stacked and supported by 6 vertical beams using half lap joints to secure in place.
