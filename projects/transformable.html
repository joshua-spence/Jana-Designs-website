Transformable Table

This side table is a bold addition for any living space. No additional materials were used to construct it, only a single cut of sheet metal. The table is practical as well as user friendly as the customer is able to personalise the table to their desired shape. My idea was to use perforations to weaken the sheet metal at four points, therefore allowing it to become more flexible.

multipurpose by design

The table is elegant and versatile which is hard to accomplish with furniture made from metal, fortunately I was able to manipulate the metal in a way that softens its appearance. The Aztec perforations add character and culture to the piece.
