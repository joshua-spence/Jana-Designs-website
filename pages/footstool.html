Footstool

The footstool was for Duresta’s Domus collection. Keeping in mind scale, detailing, functionality, colour, fabrics, manufacturing and upholstery detailing. I made the frame from the extra wood found around the workshop, welded the feet from a rectangle steel tube and sewed the upholstery from fabric supplied by Duresta.

In a living environment the footstool can be used a to rest your feet, it can be a useful surface to place your tea or keep the remote handy; and when there is a shortage of seating the footstool can comfortably seat two people. It’s multifunctional abilities make it ideal for the unexpected realities of family life.
